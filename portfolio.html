Echo Emitter

🔊 Echo Emitter Mechanic – Unreal Engine 5 The Echo Emitter is a custom mechanic designed to simulate spatial scanning through sonic waves, inspired by mechanics seen in games like Echochrome and Batman: Arkham Knight. When triggered, the player emits a visualized sonic pulse (echo ring) from their current location. This ring travels outward and interacts with the environment, visually revealing hidden paths, interactive objects, or threats within its radius.
